Into the Current

A diverse community works to conserve wildlife habitat along the central Platte River.

From settlement times to today humans have significantly altered the Platte River.

After the 1973 Endangered Species Act was invoked to ensure habitat for endangered whooping cranes...

...more than two dozen conservation groups began the arduous task of trying to restore a portion of the central Platte.
