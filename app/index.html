Every year millions of birds migrate through the central Platte Valley. Sandhill cranes take center stage.

From settlement times to today humans have significantly altered the Platte River.

After the 1973 Endangered Species Act was invoked to ensure habitat for endangered whooping cranes...

...various conservation groups began the arduous task of trying to restore a portion of the central Platte.

Here are the stories of those efforts.
