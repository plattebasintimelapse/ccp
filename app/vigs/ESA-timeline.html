Parallel Tracks

The recovery of the whooping crane (Grus americana) in the 1970s closely followed the national rise in consciousness around human impact on the environment and the need for federal environmental protection.

The battle over whooping crane habitat along the central Platte River occured while the nation's major environmental laws came into effect.
