Tips for Exploring This Story

This project is divided into three eras chronicling the evolving history of human settlement, land use and conservation work in the central Platte Valley:

  • Settlement: Early human impacts, westward expansion, homesteading, and changes in the historic river.
  • Awareness: The national rise of environmental and social consciousness through the mid-to-late twentieth century.
  • Recovery: The complex modern conservation efforts that seek to restore and preserve sections of the central Platte for wildlife.
