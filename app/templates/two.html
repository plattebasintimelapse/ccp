A Bird's Eye View

Explore the central Platte Valley through aerial imagery from the 1970s by clicking, dragging and zooming into the map.

This mosaic of aerial imagery is one of 20 georeferenced and stitched image sets completed by the USFWS in 2014. The background blue is the extent of the modern Platte River.

Zoom in to see detail. Click on the popups to read stories.

Map data courtesy USGS, USFWS & PRRIP
